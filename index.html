Our Services

What We Do

We work across multiple thematic areas to create holistic, lasting change in the communities we serve.

🏥

Healthcare Access

Providing free health camps, check-ups, eye care, and medical awareness programs for underprivileged communities.

📚

Literacy & Education

Promoting educational access, improving literacy rates, and supporting children from economically weaker sections.

👩‍💼

Women Empowerment

Skill development, menstrual health awareness, legal aid, and empowerment programs specifically for women and girls.

🌱

Livelihood Enhancement

Supporting sustainable income generation, vocational training, and entrepreneurship for marginalized populations.

💧

Water & Sanitation

Ensuring access to clean water and proper sanitation facilities in rural and underserved areas of Maharashtra.

🌿

Environmental Conservation

Promoting sustainable practices, environmental awareness, and green initiatives across all our program areas.

🐄

Animal Welfare

Supporting animal welfare initiatives and promoting compassionate treatment of animals in rural communities.

⚖️

Advocacy & Legal Aid

Championing human rights, providing legal assistance, and advocating for the rights of vulnerable communities.

About Us

Who We Are

Sanskar Vikas Foundation is a registered non-profit organization (CIN: U93030MH2012NPL237849) based in Nagpur, Maharashtra.

Our work spans multiple districts across Maharashtra, addressing critical needs in healthcare, education, women's empowerment, and rural infrastructure.
