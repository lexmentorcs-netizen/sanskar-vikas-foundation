Who We Are

A Foundation Built on Compassion

Sanskar Vikas Foundation is a registered non-profit organization incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013 (CIN: U93030MH2012NPL237849), dedicated to the holistic development of marginalized communities across Maharashtra.

Founded and led by Director Deepali Thakur, along with co-director Vikram Kamath, our foundation has grown from a grassroots initiative into a multi-program organization working across education, healthcare, women's empowerment, and environmental conservation.

We believe that sustainable social change comes from within communities — which is why our approach is community-led, participatory, and focused on building local capacity.

Our Purpose

Vision, Mission & Objectives

👁️

Our Vision

A society where every individual — regardless of caste, creed, gender, or economic status — has equal access to opportunities, dignity, and the resources needed to live a fulfilling life.

We envision communities that are self-reliant, empowered, and environmentally conscious, rooted in cultural values while embracing modern opportunities.

🚀

Our Mission

To drive sustainable, holistic development by empowering rural communities — particularly women and other marginalized groups — through quality education, accessible healthcare, and social welfare programs.

We emphasize skill development, environmental conservation, and preserving cultural values to prepare communities for an inclusive, prosperous future.

🎯

Our Objectives

  • ✓ Promote comprehensive development irrespective of caste, creed or religion
  • ✓ Foster unity, collaboration and positive engagement
  • ✓ Empower individuals for social and economic well-being
  • ✓ Build strong networks with like-minded organizations
  • ✓ Contribute to sustainable social transformation
